Oh My Summer Feastival 2019

About Us

International University Liaison Indonesia; shortened as IULI, is one the private university that was established in 2014. IULI operated in Indonesia, partnering with European Universities Consortium – EUC coordinated by TU Ilmenau (Technische Universität Ilmenau), Germany. IULI is one of the universities based on a dual academic license that is designated as a university under Indonesia’s rules and regulations. IULI was established to be a platform and bridge between Indonesia and Germany to achieve and focus on international standard that will be used for a knowledge and technologies development in university system in Indonesia.

Being a university that just only founded in 2014 did not become an obstacle for the IULI students to be active for both internal activities but also external activities. IULI empowered and required their students to not only ace in academic but also non-academic activities. One of the embodiments of this vision is the procurement of the Oh My Summer Feastival, shortened as OMS Feastival. Oh My Summer Feastival is a charity event is an enlivened by a music and arts festival accompanied by apparel bazaars, food and beverages. OMS Feastival will be held in June 2019 which contains pre-events, id est band and short movie competitions, then the closing ceremony will be enlivened by guest stars and live performances from IULI students to wrap up the entire program.
